Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Meritometrics Poster, a TikTok app used exclusively to create and post short videos to TikTok.

We do not collect or store any personal information. The only data we preserve is the TikTok Post ID for future reference for the video.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and preserve only the TikTok Post ID.

When a video is successfully posted to TikTok, the app saves only the unique Post ID (the permanent identifier assigned by TikTok to that video). This is kept solely for future reference so you can view or manage your posted videos later.

No personal data, account credentials, video content, device information, IP address, or any other information is collected or stored.

3. How Your Information Is Used

The TikTok Post ID is used exclusively to let you reference the videos you have posted through Meritometrics Poster. Nothing else is retained or used.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share the Post ID or any other data with any third parties. The Post ID is stored only on your device or in your private account within the app.

5. Data Storage

The only data ever stored by Meritometrics Poster is the TikTok Post ID. All other operations happen locally on your device or are handled directly with TikTok at the exact moment of posting. No data is retained after the Post ID is saved.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date above. Your continued use of Meritometrics Poster after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
